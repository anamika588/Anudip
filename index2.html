Festivals of Different States in India

State Festival Description
Punjab Baisakhi Celebrates the harvest season with vibrant bhangra dances and traditional feasts.
West Bengal Durga Puja A grand festival honoring Goddess Durga with elaborate pandals, music, and dance.
Kerala Onam Marks the homecoming of King Mahabali with flower carpets, boat races, and feasts.
Rajasthan Pushkar Camel Fair A colorful fair featuring camel trading, cultural events, and folk performances.
Tamil Nadu Pongal A harvest festival celebrated with traditional cooking of sweet rice and rituals.

UNESCO World Heritage Sites in India

  1. Taj Mahal, Agra: A stunning white marble mausoleum, an architectural masterpiece of Mughal art.

  2. Jaipur City, Rajasthan: A historic walled city known for its pink-hued buildings and planned architecture.

  3. Red Fort, Delhi: A grand Mughal fortress showcasing intricate designs and historical significance.

  4. Sundarbans National Park, West Bengal: A unique mangrove ecosystem, home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.
